Butter Chicken

This simplified version of the Indian classic combines chicken, tomato sauce, and a slew of aromatic spices all in one pot to make a flavorful dinner thats just as good as the version you will get at restaurants — only way easier to make. Serve it over rice with a bit of cilantro to balance the heat and dinner is done.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Chicken Food

instructions

step 1

In a large bowl, season the chicken breast with salt, pepper, 1 teaspoon of chili powder, and the teaspoon of turmeric. Let sit for 15 minutes to marinate.

step 2

Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a large pot over medium heat. Brown the chicken, then remove from the pot.

step 3

Melt another 2 tablespoons of butter in the pot, then add the onion, garam masala, remaining teaspoon of chili powder, the cumin, ginger, garlic, cayenne, cinnamon, salt and pepper. Cook until fragrant.

step 4

Add the tomato sauce and bring to a simmer. Add the water and cream and return to a simmer.

step 5

Return the chicken to the pot, cover, and simmer for another 10-15 minutes.

step 6

Stir in the last 2 tablespoons of butter and season with more salt and pepper to taste.

step 7

Serve the chicken over rice and garnish with cilantro.

step 8

Enjoy!

ingredients

1 kg boneless, skinless chicken breast, cubed

salt and pepper to taste

2 teaspoons chili powder, divided

½ teaspoon turmeric

6 tablespoons butter, divided

1 ½ cups yellow onion(225 g), diced

3 teaspoons garam masala

1 teaspoon cumin

1 teaspoon cayenne pepper

1 tablespoon ginger, grated

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 cinnamon stick

395 tomato sauce

1 cup water(240 mL)

1 cup heavy cream
